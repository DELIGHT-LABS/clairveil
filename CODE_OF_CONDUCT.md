# Code of Conduct

Clairveil contributors are expected to keep project discussions focused, respectful, and technically grounded.

Korean version: [CODE_OF_CONDUCT-kr.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT-kr.md)

## Expected Behavior

- Respect differences in experience level and areas of expertise.
- Prefer concrete technical reasoning over personal criticism.
- Keep security-sensitive reports private until maintainers coordinate disclosure.
- Make review feedback actionable.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language
- Publishing private keys, credentials, private vulnerability details, or personal information
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, discussions, or other project spaces to protect the project and contributors. Serious violations may lead to removal from project spaces.
